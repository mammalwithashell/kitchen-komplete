About Kitchen Komplete

Kitchen Komplete is the best online tool to use in the kitchen. You can search recipes in the database by what ingredients you have in your kitchen. Sign up for an account to add and remove ingredients in your Pantry. Account members can also add and share recipes between users.



User Instruction Section

Step 1: Click the Register button to create an account

Step 2: Fill out your account information and click Submit

Step 3: Go to your profile and click the pantry button

Step 4: Add items to your pantry

Step 5: Enter ingredients in the search bar to find recipes
